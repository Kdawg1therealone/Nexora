PROJECT OVERVIEW

A professional home for a Roblox development studio.

Shining Development Workspace needed a focused website capable of presenting its services and Roblox work professionally. Nexora designed and built a dark, responsive experience that combines strong typography, a bright cyan identity and clear paths to the portfolio and contact information.

The completed site explains the studio's services, displays environment, modelling and clothing work, documents its six-step development process, answers common client questions and makes its Discord contact easy to find and copy.
